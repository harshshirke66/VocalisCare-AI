# VocalisCare-AI
🌐 Website: (https://vocaliscarewebsite.vercel.app/)  

**VocalisCare AI** is an advanced healthcare AI application designed to provide comprehensive assistance through voice commands in any language. Powered by **Google Gamini**, it leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to make healthcare accessible, multilingual, and user-friendly. Developed by **Team Cybertrone**, the solution also includes features like live location-based hospital search and a medicine scanner for quick and accurate medication information.

---

## 🌟 **Features**  

1. **Voice Interaction in Multiple Languages:**  
   - Ask any healthcare-related question through voice input.  
   - Responds in the language of your choice.  
   - Supports multilingual interactions with seamless translation.  

2. **Live Location for Nearby Hospitals:**  
   - Get a list of hospitals near your current location with a single click.  
   - Uses GPS to fetch accurate real-time data.  

3. **Medicine Scanner:**  
   - Upload a photo of any medicine to get detailed information such as usage, side effects, and dosage.  
   - Provides reliable and instant information to assist users.  

4. **Powered by Google Gamini:**  
   - Utilizes the **Google Gamini AI Platform** to handle voice interactions, language processing, and image recognition efficiently.  
   - Ensures scalability, high performance, and accurate AI-driven responses.  

5. **User-Friendly Interface:**  
   - Simple and intuitive design for easy navigation.  
   - Optimized for accessibility.
